## About the Phase 2 Upgrade

### 1. When will the upgrade happen?
The upgrade will take place at block height **65500**.

### 2. How does the upgrade affect miners?
The primary impact for miners is a decrease in daily mining yield. The daily output will drop to **40%** (4/10) of what it was before the upgrade. 

Here is the breakdown:
* The total daily network output is halved (50% of the previous amount).
* However, 20% of this total output is now allocated to the "Protocol Fund".
* The remaining 80% is distributed to the miners. 
* Therefore, the actual amount of Nock miners can earn per day will be 40% of the current amount (50% * 80% = 40%).

### 3. Do I need to update my Prover?
No action is required on your part. You do not need to update your Prover.

### 4. What other changes does the upgrade bring?
* **Faster Block Time:** The block generation time has been significantly reduced. It was previously 10 minutes per block, but after the upgrade, it will be **2.5 minutes per block**.
* **Removal of Epoch Mechanism:** The Epoch mechanism has been removed, and the network difficulty now adjusts in real-time. Consequently, calculating the theoretical yield for 1 p/s has become more difficult because the difficulty fluctuates much faster.
